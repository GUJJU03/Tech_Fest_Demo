Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is a popular mobile battle royale game developed by Krafton. Launched as a rebranded version of PUBG Mobile for the Indian audience, BGMI has quickly gained immense popularity. Here's a brief overview:
Overview: BGMI offers an immersive battle royale experience where players compete against each other until only one survives. The game is set in a virtual battleground where 100 players parachute onto an island and scavenge for weapons and equipment to eliminate opponents.

Valorant is a tactical first-person shooter game developed and published by Riot Games. Released in 2020, Valorant has quickly become a popular title in the esports scene. Combining precise gunplay with unique agent abilities, players engage in intense 5v5 matches to secure objectives and outsmart their opponents. With a diverse cast of agents, each possessing distinct abilities, Valorant offers a dynamic and strategic gameplay experience. Whether you're a sharpshooter or a master tactician, Valorant provides a thrilling competitive environment that demands skill, teamwork, and strategic thinking.

Free Fire, developed by Garena, is a fast-paced battle royale game that has taken the mobile gaming world by storm. Released in 2017, Free Fire has amassed a massive player base due to its exciting gameplay and constant updates. In this 10-minute survival showdown, 50 players parachute onto an island, scavenging for weapons and resources. The shrinking play zone intensifies the action, forcing players into close encounters and strategic battles.
What sets Free Fire apart is its unique character system, allowing players to choose from a diverse roster of characters, each with their own special abilities. Whether it's healing teammates, moving stealthily, or gaining a speed boost, these abilities add an extra layer of strategy to the game.
